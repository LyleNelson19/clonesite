About McCall

McCall, Idaho is a resort town located roughly 2 hours north of the Boise area and situated next to Payette Lake. McCall is a popular tourist destination for residents of Idaho. Tourists come up here in the summer to escape the heat, to view the scenery, and to enjoy outdoor activities such as hiking, swimming, and kayaking. Many people also come here during winter to go skiing at the Brundage and Tamarack ski resorts.
